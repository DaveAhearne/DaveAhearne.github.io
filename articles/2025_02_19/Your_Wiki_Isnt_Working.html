Your wiki isn't working.

In your workplace you have a wiki, any self respecting business more than a handful of people does. People refer to it all the time, they say things like "Oh you can find this in the wiki", or "Yeah there's a page for this in the wiki!"

Except for some reason, using it sucks.

Hoarding isn't helping

One of the typical reactions that people have in the workplace is to put everything in the wiki somewhere, had a conversation with someone? Wiki. Creating a new service? Wiki. Ate something for breakfast? Wiki.
